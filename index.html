Welcome to Wallingford Walkers

Wallingford Walkers provides the best care your dog will receive West of the cascades. The business was founded by young entrepreneur Rose Hunskor when she realized her passion for dogs. Your dog will have the experience of its life, whether they are strolling through the streets of Wallingford or romping along the Oak Harbor beach. If you are looking for the perfect retreat for your dog, choose Wallingford Walkers.

Wallingford Walkers provides a variety of services for your dogs needs .
